Veggie Stir-Fry with Tofu


Recipe Description

This flavorful and colorful stir-fry makes a nutritious and satisfying meal. Enjoy experimenting with different vegetables and sauces to suit your taste preferences!


Ingredients

  • 1 block firm tofu, pressed and cubed
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons saseme oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 cup snow peas, trimmed
  • 2 carrots, julienned
  • 4 green onions, sliced
  • Cooked rice or noodles for serving

Steps

  1. Prepare Tofu: Press the tofu to remove excess moisture by wrapping it in a clean kitchen towel and placing a heavy object on top for 20-30 minutes. Cube the tofu.
  2. Marinate Tofu: n a bowl, mix the cubed tofu with 1 tablespoon of soy sauce. Let it marinate for 15-20 minutes.
  3. Stir-Fry: Heat 1 tablespoon of sesame oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat. Add the marinated tofu and cook until golden brown on all sides. Remove the tofu from the pan and set it aside.
  4. Vegetable Mix: In the same pan, add the remaining sesame oil. Stir in garlic and ginger and cook for a minute until fragrant. Add bell peppers, broccoli, snow peas, and carrots. Stir-fry for 4-5 minutes until vegetables are tender yet crisp.
  5. Combine and Season: Return the tofu to the pan with the veggies. Add the remaining soy sauce and toss everything together. Sprinkle sliced green onions over the stir-fry.
  6. Serve: Serve the veggie stir-fry with tofu over cooked rice or noodles.
